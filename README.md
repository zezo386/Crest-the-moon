# Crest the moon
this is a game about a traveler who gets lost and tries to find a way out
this is an RPG game 
# the game lore
this game is about a traveler that gets lost and finds 2 paths

he uses items and swords to defeat bandits in forests
and gold to buy newer swords and potions and other items to improve his performance aginist the bandits
along the way he discovers new villages and learns more about this mysterious place

his mission is to return back to his main city after finding the shore and building a ship and having a map 

where he will encounter pirates, sea monsters, and other monsters along the way while also needing to rest in nearby islands

after returning his story lives on for a long time as the man who came back after his memorial
and he becomes a symbol of patience and hard work in his village
# game mechanics
## interactive console
this game uses an interactive console for desicion making so you can use up and down arrow keys to navigate the options and enter to choose it while also being updated frame by frame and using the console only without any gui libraries
## bandit fights mechanic
when going through a forest there is a chance that you will encounter a bandit on the way 

these bandits will try to steal your gold and if you lose you return back to the last checkpoint

you can fight them and have a chance to win

you can attack them doing damage according the sword/weapon you are using while also having some extra parameters to it
the damage is calculated as the following

player damage = (player sword damage * (random number between 0.75 and 1.25))

you can also defend which reduces the enemies damage by 80% while you heal a random percentage of you health randing from 5 to 15% hp

you can also attepmt to flee
so you have a 35% chance to flee succecfully but if you dont flee succesfully you take 1.5 more damage than normal damage you would usually take

after your turn the bandit starts his turn and will attack you
